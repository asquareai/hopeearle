DAVID DE ALWIS
Partner

David practices in family law and has acted in a variety of matters ranging from the simple to the most complex. 

He has particularly acted in matters involving:

  • Complex financial settlements with large and complicated asset pools;

  • Sensitive parenting matters;

  • Third party family law matters;

  • Maintenance and child support issues; and

  • Matters involving the Hague Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction. 

In addition, he regularly provides advice in relation to family violence issues to both applicants and respondents in intervention order matters

David has presented papers in family law at a number of international forums and is a member of LawAsia, the Asian Australian Lawyer's Association and the Law Institute of Victoria. 

His expertise as a family lawyer is enhanced by his strategic and commercial approach to family law matters and his ability to represent a variety of clients from various backgrounds. 

David’s Asian heritage and working knowledge of Sinhalese makes him particularly sensitive to the issues facing his clients from an Asian background. 

David is also a Registered Migration Agent and is able to act in a variety of complex immigration matters.
