Laura is currently completing her Juris Doctor at Monash University Law School, having graduated from RMIT University in 2014 with a Bachelor of Criminal Justice Administration with Distinction. Laura started at Hope Earle in 2017 as a Notary Paralegal and now assists staff generally as a Law Clerk & Property Paralegal.

Laura works closely with property lawyer Evelyn Wong. Together, they handle all aspects of purchaser and vendor matters for residential, commercial and off-the-plan properties, from contract stage to successful settlement. Laura has proficiency across PEXA and Duties Online platforms and has experience handling the following:

  • Day to day handling of property conveyances;

  • Preparation of Contracts of Sale and Vendor Statements;

  • Conducting searches and preparing due diligence reports;

  • Preparation and lodgement of State Revenue Office stamp duty refund applications, duty statements and complex assessments;

  • Preparation of section 27 statements and GST Withholding Notices; and

  • Preparation and lodgement of Transmission, Lost Title and Subdivision Applications.

Laura also assists our partners and senior lawyers across a range of commercial matters, including:

  • Preparation of briefs, various court documents and discovery for a range of commercial proceedings heard in State and Federal Courts;

  • Preparation of VCAT applications and related documents;

  • Preparation of adjudication responses for Building and Construction disputes in Victoria and Queensland;

  • Maintenance and transfer of domestic and international trade marks;

  • Maintenance and transfer of Australian Business Names; and

  • Use of the ASIC companies’ portal to manage registered company particulars and shareholder information.

Laura also provides accounts and administrative support as well as having carriage of various marketing tasks.
